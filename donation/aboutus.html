ABOUT US

What are we?

Non profit fundraising website that allows one to contact the association for help as well as allows one to make donations via funds, clothing, food etc.

How do we function?

We mainly work in 4 sectors that are Natural calamities, Education, NGOs and medical aids. Donations are made on the basis of these four sectors and then the amounts are funded to the needy in these sectors. People who are in need of donations or other help can also contact us and we consider them too for further donations and help. Non-capital donations can also be made by dropping by the donation stuff at our given address
